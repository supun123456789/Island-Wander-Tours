Udawalawe National Park

About Udawalawe National Park

Udawalawe is one of the best places in Sri Lanka to see elephants in the wild. The park is also home to deer, crocodiles, peacocks, and water buffalo.

Highlights

  • Close-up views of elephants
  • Beautiful landscapes and grasslands
  • Birdwatching paradise
  • Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home

Tips for Visitors

Visit early morning or late afternoon for best sightings. Bring binoculars and sun protection.

Location

Located in the south of Sri Lanka, about 165 km from Colombo.
