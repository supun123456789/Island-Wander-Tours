About Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as "Little England," is a picturesque town in Sri Lankaâ€™s central highlands. It's known for its cool climate, colonial architecture, and tea plantations.

What to See

  • Gregory Lake and park activities

Travel Advice

Bring warm clothes as it can be chilly, especially at night. Visit between March and May for the best weather and blooming flowers.

Location

Central Province, around 180 km from Colombo via Kandy or Hatton.
