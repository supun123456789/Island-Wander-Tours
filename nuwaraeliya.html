Nuwara Eliya

About Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as "Little England," is a scenic highland city known for its cool climate, lush greenery, colonial charm, and tea plantations. It's a popular getaway for those seeking fresh mountain air and peaceful surroundings.

Highlights

  • Gregory Lake and boat rides
  • Horton Plains and World's End trek
  • Tea factory visits and plantations
  • Victoria Park and Hakgala Botanical Garden

Tips for Visitors

Bring warm clothing as temperatures can be quite cool, especially in the evenings. Plan a visit during the April season for the flower festival and horse races.

Location

Nuwara Eliya is located in the Central Province of Sri Lanka, approximately 180 km from Colombo.
