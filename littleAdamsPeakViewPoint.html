Little Adam's Peak View Point

About Little Adam's Peak

A popular hike in Ella, offering stunning panoramic views of the valley, tea plantations, and Ella Rock. Named after the sacred Adam’s Peak due to its shape.

Highlights

  • Easy 30–40 minute hike
  • Great for sunrise or sunset
  • Photo spots and swing viewpoints

Tips for Visitors

Wear walking shoes and bring water. No entrance fee. Start early for cooler temperatures.

Location

Located in Ella, Central Highlands of Sri Lanka.
