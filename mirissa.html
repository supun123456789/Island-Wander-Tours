Mirissa Beach

About Mirissa

Mirissa is a popular beach town on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, known for its golden sand beaches, vibrant nightlife, and whale watching excursions. It is a favorite among both backpackers and families.

Top Experiences

  • Whale and dolphin watching tours (seasonal)
  • Surfing and bodyboarding
  • Beachfront cafes and seafood restaurants
  • Secret Beach and Parrot Rock Bridge
