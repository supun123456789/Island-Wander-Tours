Ella

About Ella

Ella is a small town nestled in the lush green hills of Sri Lanka’s Central Highlands. Famous for its scenic beauty, tea plantations, and relaxing atmosphere, Ella is a favorite among nature lovers and hikers.

Highlights

  • Scenic train ride from Kandy to Ella
  • Hike to Little Adam’s Peak for panoramic views
  • Marvel at the Nine Arches Bridge
  • Visit Ravana Falls and Ravana Cave

Tips for Visitors

Start your hikes early to avoid the heat. The area is best explored over 2–3 days to enjoy the slow-paced atmosphere. Don’t miss the sunrise view from Little Adam’s Peak.

Location

Ella is located in the Badulla District, about 200 km from Colombo, in the central highlands of Sri Lanka.
